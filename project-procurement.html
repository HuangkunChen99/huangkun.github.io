Project Overview

This project focused on analyzing over $1.7B in procurement and tail-spend data across 10K+ global suppliers to identify savings opportunities and streamline sourcing strategies. Our insights were presented at the 2025 INFORMS Analytics Conference.

Key Contributions

  • Cleaned and standardized 210K+ rows of spend data from multiple sources
  • Built interactive Tableau dashboards tracking compliance, pricing, and supplier performance
  • Identified 50%+ supplier consolidation opportunities with $40M in projected savings
  • Applied NLP to categorize suppliers and evaluate contract language risks

Results

The analysis uncovered inefficiencies in indirect spend and empowered stakeholders with visualized KPIs and actionable strategies. It led to executive-level changes in vendor engagement and sourcing policy.
